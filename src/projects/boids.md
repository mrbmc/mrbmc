I have always found beauty in nature. 

The "Boids" Algorithm is a simulation of flocking behavior found in fish and fowl. The algorithm tracks the position, and velocity, of each "boid" and aligns their course without colliding just as flocking animals do.

I think the resulting patterns are mesmerizing.
