Design & Product Leader

I deeply believe that putting people at the center of everything you do - as a leader, as a designer, as an entrpreneur - leads to good work and good business outcomes.

Most recently I led Enterprise product design at for our streaming services Disney+, Hulu, STAR+, and ESPN+. Previously I led design for Growth (propelling Disney+ & ESPN+ to 130M subscribers in 2 years!), UX Research and Sports Products. Before joining Disney, I built high performing teams at startups, and enterprises using a customer-first mindset and cross-functional collaboration.

My leadership approach is based on my core values of curiosity and authenticity. I have also been told that I excel at inspirational leadership and product strategy; but the work I’m most proud of is expanding design’s business impact and mentoring design leaders.
